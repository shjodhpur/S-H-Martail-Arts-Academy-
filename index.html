About Coach Abdul Wajid

Coach Abdul Wajid is a Self-Defense Diploma Holder and a 3rd Dan Kukkiwon Black Belt in Taekwondo. He also holds a 1st Dan Black Belt in Self-Defense and currently serves as the Rajasthan Taekkyon Secretary. He has participated and showcased his skills at various recognized open-level and national martial arts events, earning respect for his discipline and technique. With years of experience in training students, Coach Abdul Wajid focuses on building confidence, self-control, and respect through martial arts.

Motto: Train hard, stay humble, and rise strong.
